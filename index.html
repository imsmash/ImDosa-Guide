QHow do you teleport so far?
It's C (Cloud General) into RMB (Virga Shaft). Cloud General semi-randomly picks a target and will try to knock them back - there are some weird mechanics about it I won't get into. But there's a specific interaction where after using Cloud General, your Virga Shaft distance is greatly increased. If you do it correctly it can teleport you a massive distance, and yes - even vertically up terrain. There is a max distance and the terrain plays a huge role in how far you can go. Best advice I can offer is getting a friend and testing out how far you can go on different levels of terrain to get a feel for it yourself.
QHow do you manage your stamina?
S F (Cloud Shield) costs zero stamina - use it constantly. Weave in F F and Spacebar between movements: F F regens stamina while keeping you in SA on a low cooldown, and Spacebar doesn't use stamina but helps you keep moving forward. Avoid spamming LMB (Velum Slash) just to move - drains stamina fast and goes unprotected on cooldown.
QI keep getting CC'd out of S F (Cloud Shield), help!
It's best to use S F after you use some other skill to break the distance or grant yourself some protection. For example, using A/D RMB (Flow: Cloud Drift), Shift A/D (Stratus Shift), or Shift Z (Moonlit Haze) before S F can help reduce the risk of getting hit accelerated out of S F and the move ending early. However, frame drops can still happen and cause it. Note this isn't a 100% guarantee it won't happen - it's just to mitigate it.
QWhat does Q (Prime: Wind Shear) do?
Prime: Wind Shear is enhanced by the skill enhancement Loop: Wind Shear. When you successfully block a skill with it, you heal 1000HP and enter SA with a 99% DR bonus. At the same time it knocks back nearby enemies - you can immediately follow up with RMB (Virga Shaft) to knock down any knocked back targets. The AoE on the knockback is pretty large so it can catch multiple people.
The other thing worth knowing is that Q acts as a cancel for almost any animation. Mid-cast into Shift E? Press Q to cancel it. Stuck in the F F animation? Press Q to get out. It's one of the most useful tools on the kit for staying fluid and not getting caught in a bad animation.
